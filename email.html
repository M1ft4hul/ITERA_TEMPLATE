By default, you will receive notifications when any action completes. But you can configure the notifications to only be sent when problems happen. Or you can configure the notifications to never be sent.

In addition, the notifications can be sent:

  • To alternate email addresses,
  • To a Slack channel,
  • To a HipChat channel,
  • As a Tweet, or
  • As a message to an Amazon SNS topic
